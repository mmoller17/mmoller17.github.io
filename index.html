Maura is an artist and designer.

In my work I endeavour to be innovative and accommodative. I want my designs to be simple, effective, and intuitive. I want them to be durable; made of quality, natural materials; to help the environment; and improve quality of life in a sustainable way. My designs should be accessible and inviting to a broad range of people, simple-to-use and with world-wide appeal.

During the design process I find the most important quality is being adaptable. I try not to give up on a project when the going gets tough, and instead come at it from a different angle. Something else I think is important is an international-oriented mindset.
